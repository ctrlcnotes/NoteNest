🪺NoteNest

NoteNest is your cozy campus for collaborative studying.

We built NoteNest to turn exam prep into a creative studio: students swap summaries, doodles, and “aha!” moments with instant comments and reactions. It’s perfect for anyone who learns best in community—from lab partners to literature lovers.

What is NoteNest?

A shared wall of student-made notes, reactions, and threads that feel as friendly as group study but as organized as your favorite planner.

Who is it for?

High school and university learners, clubs, and study buddies who crave a visual, social way to keep coursework clear.

Why we built it

Because focused studying shouldn’t feel lonely. Every shared note turns into a ripple of understanding!

Study Flow

  • 🎯 Introduce yourself with a fun profile.
  • 🪄 Drop highlights, sketches, or mnemonics.
  • 💬 Follow up with questions, comments, and emoji vibes.
  • 🗂️ Edit or delete anytime to keep things fresh.
